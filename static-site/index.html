About JSS Polytechnic for Women

JSS Polytechnic for Women, established in 1983, has been at the forefront of providing quality technical education exclusively for women. Located in the cultural city of Mysuru, the institution is committed to empowering women through technical education and developing skilled professionals.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing quality technical education and creating competent women professionals with ethical values.

Our Mission

To impart quality technical education through innovative teaching-learning processes, and to nurture creativity and research capabilities.

Our Campus

Sprawling campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern laboratories, well-stocked library, and various facilities for all-round development of students.
